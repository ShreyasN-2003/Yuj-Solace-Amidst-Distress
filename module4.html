HOW TO MANAGE TIME IN THE BUSY LIFE OF JOB FROM 9 TO 5 ?

Here is the solution for your problem.

We follow some techniques to manage time, They are :

  • 1. Promodono Technique.
  • 2. Eisenhower Technique.
  • 3. Parkinson Technique.

1.Promodono Technique

The promodoro technique is a time management method based on 2s-minutes of focused work broken by five minutes break.

2.Eisenhower Technique

The Eisenhower Matrix, also referred to as Urgent-Important Matrix, helps you decide on and prioritize tasks by urgency and importance, sorting out less urgent and important tasks which you should either delegate or not do at all.

3.Parkinson Technique

British historian Cyril Northcote Parkinson became famous for the phrase “work expands so as to fill the time available for its completion.” In other words, the amount of time you give yourself to complete a specific task is the amount of time it will take you to complete that task.

How Parkinson Technique Works?

  • Try working without a computer charger.
    This will force you to finish a project before your computer dies.
  • Get it done early.
    Instead of finishing an essay by midnight, try to get it done by noon.
  • Set a deadline.
    Give yourself a set time to do something—and then cut it in half.
  • Limit time for tasks.
    Give yourself only 20 minutes in the morning to answer emails.
